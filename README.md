## Hi! I'm William Wei 👋

- I graduated from New York University (NYU) in 2025 with a Bachelor's in Biomolecular Science and minors in Computer Science and Mathematics.

- I’m currently working as a Machine Learning Research Assistant at [Smart Microphysiological Systems for Precision Medicine](https://engineering.nyu.edu/research/student-research/vertically-integrated-projects/vip-teams/smart-microphysiological-system-for-precision-medicine-gy) at New York University developing an Attention-LSTM to evaluate patient responsiveness to immunotherapy for pancreatic cancer.

- At the [Garetz and Hartman Research Laboratory](https://wp.nyu.edu/hartmanlab/category/people/), I developed a YOLOv11-based CNN to track and automatically measure the shape and size of microscopic crystals produced by non-photochemical laser-induced nucleation (NPLIN).

- I've also deployed a full-stack website educational website ([physicalchem.org](https://physicalchem.org/)) with interactive simulations of quantum mechanics to help students interact with concepts while working as a course assistant for New York University's Department of Chemical and Biomolecular Engineering.

- And a small desktop app [TranslateResearch](https://github.com/william-wei0/TranslateResearch)for summarizing and translating research papers using Anthropic and OpenAI, with multilingual translation, a light-weight RAG system, PDF text extraction, and persistent summary history. Built with Electron, React, and the Anthropic and OpenAI APIs to help students translate research papers to their native language. Also implemented hallucination checks to ensure accurate summaries and preserve technical terms.
